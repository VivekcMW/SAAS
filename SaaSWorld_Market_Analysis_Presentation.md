## Slide 3: Executive Summary
- SaaSWorld is a next-gen SaaS marketplace for business software discovery
- Built with Nuxt 3, TypeScript, and modern web tech
- Focus: Superior UX, performance, and mobile-first design
- Target: SMBs and growth-focused businesses
- Opportunity: $170B+ global SaaS market, growing 15-20% annually

## Slide 4: Market Overview
- Global SaaS market: $170B+ (2024)

- 50M+ SMBs worldwide as addressable market

- Average company uses 110+ SaaS apps
